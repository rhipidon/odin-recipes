A delicious breaded slice of veal loin usually served with salad and fries, the Cotoletta is a typical dish of northern Italy, particularly Milan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bone-in veal loin about 250 g of each about 5 mm thick (alternatively, you can choose 2 slices of boneless veal)
  • 2 small eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • 150 g clarified butter
  • Maldon-type salt flakes

Steps:

  1. First with the help of a meat tenderizer flatten the slices a little so that they are about the same thickness.
  2. Then coat each cutlet in breadcrumbs, then in beaten egg and finally in breadcrumbs again, in order to obtain a homogeneous breadcrumb coating that is present in every part. Then press down well.
  3. Then heat a large frying pan with clarified butter. Wait until it melts to a not too high temperature and cook your ribs about 3 minutes per side. extend by 1 minute per side if you want them well done. Be sure to drizzle the bone with the hot butter as well with a spoon so that the meat cooks evenly everywhere. Reduce the cooking time to 1 minute per side if you have thin slices, or increase the time if your slices are quite thick . The end result must be a golden - caramelized color.
  4. Finally dust with salt and serve hot; in this case with arugula and cherry tomatoes previously seasoned with a drizzle of oil and salt.
  5. Here is your Cotoletta alla Milanese ready!
